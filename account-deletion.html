Delete Account

Last updated: December 30, 2025

You can request deletion of your Oxia account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a deletion request and what data will be removed.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, send an email request following these steps:

  1. Send an email to: drekza@yandex.ru
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Send the request from the same email address you used for Google Sign-In

Important: For security purposes, we can only process deletion requests sent from the email address registered to your account. Requests from other email addresses will not be processed.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account information (email, display name)
  • Smoking statistics and progress data
  • Your personal reasons for quitting
  • All other data associated with your account

Data Stored Locally

Some data is stored only on your device and is not affected by account deletion:

  • Emergency contacts
  • App theme preferences

To remove this data, uninstall the app from your device.

Processing Time

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at:

Email: drekza@yandex.ru
