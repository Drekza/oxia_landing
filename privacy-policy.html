Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Oxia ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application designed to help users quit smoking. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (from your Google account)

Smoking Data

To provide our core functionality, we collect information you provide about your smoking habits:

  • Smoking statistics and progress tracking
  • Your personal reasons for quitting

Local Data

Some data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Emergency contacts (up to 3 contacts for the "call a loved one" feature)
  • App theme preferences

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, and Performance Monitoring to improve the app. These services collect:

  • Anonymous usage events (such as screen views and feature usage)
  • Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This analytics data does not include personally identifiable information and cannot be linked to your account.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Track your progress toward quitting smoking
  • Improve app stability and performance

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not send marketing emails.

Data Storage and Security

Your account and smoking data is stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union (Ireland). We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission and secure authentication.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase — for data storage (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring — for anonymous analytics and crash reporting (Firebase Privacy Policy)

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Oxia is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
